## 🔥 The Mid-Season Shakeup (the fun part)

The back half of Season 0 goes off the rails — **on purpose.** For the rest of the season:

- **Wins & losses swing 4× harder.** The K-factor jumps to 160 — one big upset can reorder the board.
- **The floor drops to 500.** Plenty of room to fall… and to climb.
- **+100 comeback buffer.** Anyone who's played and sits under 1000 gets a one-time +100 boost. A fresh shot at the pack.
- **Every loss counts.** During the shakeup, a close loss stings as much as a blowout — top dogs can't coast on tight games anymore.

Translation: **the top is no longer safe, and the bottom is no longer hopeless.** Go play.

> ⚠️ This is a **temporary** stress test, just for fun. The "real" scoring system — the [Season 0.5 margin-as-score model](season-0.5-elo-update.md), where close games barely move and blowouts move the most — returns for official play in Season 1.
